Design Or Convert Your Kindle Covers To Acx And Audio Book











About:

Get your Kindle and Createspace Books converted into Audio Book and ACX Cover stress free and get accepted instantly!



Have you just published your book or have been a seasoned author and want your book to be available on the Audible platform as well? Look no further, I can help you convert your present Kindle book cover into and acceptable ACX cover.



Here are what you will get in the design package:



The conversion will meet the ACX requirements 



  • Contain both the name of the title and author(s).
  • No smaller than 2400 x 2400 pixels in size.

  • more than 72 dpi resolution 

  • A true squared cover

  • No colored borders on the side
  • 24-bit images

  • No Jewel cases, promotional stickers etc
  • No Pornographic and offensive materials


If you do not have the Source file with you please contact me before place the order.
