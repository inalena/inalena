Design A Minimalistic Cover











About:

Are you looking for a minimalistic custom made poster design for your movie, event or business? Then you are in the right place. 

Let's get your message across, supported by an expressive poster.

Too often, posters are overcrowded with information, but low in visual value, which leads to the audience miss the point. Long, hard-to-read blocks of text are not only uninteresting, but also difficult to read.



WHAT YOU WILL GET

Why using a minimalistic poster design?

  • Less is more, a well-designed poster supports to underline the key elements of information given
  • clean and minimalistic design as PDF file



WHAT I NEED FROM YOU

What do you need to get started?

- required: script/text/information

- not required but desirable: logo and image(s) you want to show (please notice that commercial use requires that all used images be properly licensed)
