Design Professional Book Cover











About:

The book cover is the most important single element of a book to promote it over your potential readers. Therefore having an attractive cover that reflects the content of the book is paramount in making sales.



Here, I will help you in designing such an attractive book cover for your book/ebook. This is a high-end service where you will get a premium style book cover for an affordable price.



The ebook or book cover you will get;



  • Develop from the scratch (NO copied content), therefore unique only for you
  • Provide in high-quality PDF file format
  • Editable layered PSD file included (You can change the text or layout later)
  • 3D design of your book cover included
  • Branding guide will be included (Color palette and fonts)


I expect you to provide:



  1. The size of the book cover
  2. The text you want to display on your cover
  3. Any special images you have including logos
  4. Any colors and fonts you like to use 
  5. If you have a design in mind please draw it and send


Please don't hesitate to ask me at any time if you have any queries.
